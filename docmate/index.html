About DocMate

Why We Built This

Applying for government entrance exams, academic admissions, or jobs should be about showcasing your qualifications—not wrestling with stubborn portal upload requirements. Every day, thousands of applicants face high-stakes deadlines, only to be blocked by rigid document rules: "Passport photo must be under 20KB," or "PDF certificate must not exceed 200KB." We built this platform to bridge a frustrating gap. While generic online image editors force users through trial-and-error cycles to find the right compression levels, our utility delivers mathematical certainty. We eliminate the technical guesswork so you can submit your forms confidently on the first try.

Our Core Philosophy

We firmly believe your personal data should remain yours. Unlike legacy conversion platforms that upload your files to remote cloud servers, our architecture processes your files entirely in-browser. Your photos, IDs, and certificates never leave your machine, ensuring complete data security and instantaneous speeds.

Fast. Accurate. Private.

Your documents, your device. Secure, target-size compression for error-free submissions.
